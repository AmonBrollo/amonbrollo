My Favorite Things

  • Ali Abdaal is a productivity youtuber who has the highest quality content for free out there. I'm a fan of the podcast he does with his brother and his other podcast where he delves in the mind of successful people. And his Skillshare courses are topnotch.
  • Stoicism is a philosophy that was practiced 2000 years ago and proves to be useful even in our days. To get into it I recommend reading Obstacle is the Way by Ryan Holiday or Meditation by Marcus Aurelius.
  • Hamilton Broadway musical is a sung-and-rapped-through musical about the story of the American Founding Father Alexander Hamilton. After listening to this the first time, I could not listen to anything else for weeks. It could have been just the political and historical elements of it, but the music and spectacle are phenomenal! I could not recommend it more.
